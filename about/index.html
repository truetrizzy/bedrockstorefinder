About Pickaxe Studios

Pickaxe Studios is a Minecraft Marketplace creator building premium addons, worlds, skin packs, texture packs, and mashups for Minecraft Bedrock Edition. With over 50 products, 100,000+ downloads, and a 4.5+ star average rating, we are one of the top-rated creator teams on the Minecraft Marketplace.

Our Mission

We believe Minecraft is at its best when players have access to high-quality, well-maintained content. Our mission is to create the best addons, worlds, and content packs for Minecraft Bedrock Edition — products that are fun, polished, regularly updated, and accessible to players on every platform.

What We Create

Pickaxe Studios builds content across every category on the Minecraft Marketplace:

  • Addons — Skyblock survival, lucky blocks, custom mobs, new weapons, furniture, and utility mods that add new gameplay mechanics to Minecraft.
  • Worlds — One block survival challenges, PvP arenas, adventure maps, dropper courses, parkour levels, and mini-game collections.
  • Skin Packs — Themed character skin collections including medieval, sci-fi, fantasy, and seasonal designs.
  • Texture Packs — HD texture overhauls, themed visual packs, and performance-optimized resource packs.
  • Mashups — Complete themed experiences combining custom worlds, skins, and textures into a unified package.

Our Commitment to Quality

Every Pickaxe Studios product goes through extensive testing before release. We test on all Bedrock platforms (Windows, iOS, Android, Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch) and in both singleplayer and multiplayer modes. Our average rating of 4.5+ stars across thousands of reviews reflects our commitment to delivering content that works and that players enjoy.

Regular Updates

We do not release a product and forget about it. When Minecraft releases a new version, we update our products within days to ensure compatibility. We also actively add new content — our Skyblock Ultimate addon has received 12 content updates in the past year, and our Lucky Block addon regularly gets new outcomes added. Players who purchase a Pickaxe Studios product get ongoing value through free updates.

Community Feedback

We read every review and take player feedback seriously. Many of our most popular features started as player suggestions. If you have feedback or ideas for our products, we want to hear from you.

Find Us on the Minecraft Marketplace

All Pickaxe Studios products are sold exclusively through the official Minecraft Marketplace. Open Minecraft Bedrock Edition on any platform, go to the Marketplace, and search for "Pickaxe Studios" to browse our full catalog.
