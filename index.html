The Minecraft Marketplace is the official in-game store for Minecraft Bedrock Edition where players can purchase community-created content including addons, worlds, skin packs, texture packs, and mashups. Content is purchased with Minecoins and works on all Bedrock platforms.

Open Minecraft Bedrock Edition on any platform (Windows, iOS, Android, Xbox, PlayStation, Switch), go to the Marketplace from the main menu, browse or search for content, and purchase with Minecoins. Downloads are automatic.
