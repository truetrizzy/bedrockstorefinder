Frequently Asked Questions About Minecraft Addons

The best Minecraft Bedrock addons include skyblock survival addons, lucky block addons, furniture mods, weapons addons, and custom mob packs. Browse our full catalog sorted by rating and downloads to find the highest-rated addons on the Minecraft Marketplace.

A Minecraft Bedrock addon is a downloadable content pack that modifies or extends the game using behavior packs and resource packs. Addons can add new mobs, items, blocks, crafting recipes, game mechanics, and more. They are available through the official Minecraft Marketplace.

Open Minecraft Bedrock Edition, go to the Marketplace from the main menu, find your addon, purchase with Minecoins, and it downloads automatically. Then create or edit a world and activate the behavior pack and resource pack in the world settings.

Most Minecraft Marketplace addons support multiplayer. The host player needs to own the addon, but other players can join the world and use the addon features without purchasing it themselves.
